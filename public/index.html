English

Gizlilik Politikası

English

1. Information Collection: SaveStream does not require or collect any personal details, registration, or contact information. All queried links are processed purely dynamically to fetch metadata from original social media servers.

2. Usage: Quoted URLs and temporary download cache files are only stored temporarily on the server during the active downloading process and are automatically cleaned up immediately after delivery to the user.

3. Cookies: We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. Any local configuration cookies are used strictly to maintain session identifiers for background downloads.

4. Third-Party Platforms: All video files, thumbnails, and media streams belong to their respective original hosting platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram). SaveStream does not host or claim rights to any of these media contents.
